evolution_of_light

A roguelike dungeon crawler written in python. The objective of the game is to progress your character through the "evolution of light", from lightning to LEDs. A demo of the game, along with the source and other technical information can be found at bitbucket.org/kmanbrusse/evolution_of_light/.
